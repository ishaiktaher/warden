Identity and authorization infrastructure

Give AI agents authority—not unlimited access.

Warden sits between agent runtimes and real tools. It independently verifies identity, delegated authority, policy and approval before credentials are resolved or an external side effect occurs.

Bounded delegation

Short-lived capabilities bind principal, agent, run, action, resource and expiry. Child authority can only narrow.

Credentials stay out of prompts

Secrets remain in operator-selected custody and are injected only at the connector boundary after an allow decision.

Fail-closed enforcement

Revocation, replay protection, approvals, rate limits, kill switches and tamper-evident auditing run outside the model.

This hosted surface is read-only and contains no production credentials. Deploy the control plane using the operator checklist in docs/PRODUCTION.md.
